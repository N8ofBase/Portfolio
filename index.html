About Me

My name is Nate Sloan. I have been married to an amazing woman since 2010 and we have been licensed foster parents since 2016. I have been a massage therapist since 2012 and owned my own massage studio until I had to close it in 2020 due to the pandemic. I have been a life long musician and gamer, and when I lost my business I went online and took classes for C#, Unity, and game design. After that I was accepted and graduated from LaunchCode's first independent course certification for JavaScript and Java. Now I am in the process of building my own games in hopes of being able to bring joy to others. Please click the links below to view some of my game design documents, ideas, and D&D campaign creative writings. Thank you for your interest.

The Icosahedron is a turn based, dice rolling, choose your own adventure fantasy game where the player must navigate through 20 unique worlds, solving puzzles, and helping other beings in need.

I Crit You Not is a video game framework I built before The Icosahedron but have merged it into another aspect of that game. It allows players to play through a campaign I wrote inspired by tabletop dungeons and dragons called The Terror on Talus

The Terror on Talus is the documentation of a 2 year long dungeons and dragons campaign that I wrote and played through with my friends and became the story line for tier 2 of The Icosahedron.
