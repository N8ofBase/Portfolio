Where's Your Shampoo Mustache?

This is the debut album by The Shampoo Sharks released in 2001, 3 years after we formed in 1999. We were just teenagers at the time but through the help of our families we were able to professionally record this at Smith Lee Studios in Maplewood. It is very silly and fun. Don't forget about the strange radio theater we did for the secret songs at the end of the album. Hope this brings back some good memories.
