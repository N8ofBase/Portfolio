This is Therapy

This is the final album from POSTCARD in 2009. While we were recording in the studio, Merrick's mother died from a brain tumor. "This is Therapy" truly was therapy for us and hopefully you can use it today to overcome your stress and struggles by headbanging vigorously.

Victory is Ours

This is the first album as POSTCARD. We recorded it in my basement with a pirated version of Pro-Tools that Merrick basically taught himself how to use. The audio quality isn't the greatest for that reason, but you can get an idea of our raw passion, musicianship, and song writing skills. Now that I listen back to it, it was pretty amazing what we were able to create in our basement. Enjoy the debut POSTCARD EP, Victory is Ours!

Volume & Glad You're Gone

Glad You're Gone was released first, but later we rerecorded all the same songs plus a few more for Volume so I am just including Volume for the time being. I actually don't remember much about the recording of these albums but I do remember collaborating with my childhood friend Pat who had just gotten into videography, and he made two music videos with us for My New Favorite Color in 2006, and Make it Hard in 2007, which was a really great experience.
